Small pill label for highlighting a plan, a status, or a count.

```jsx
<Badge tone="accent">Популярный</Badge>
<Badge tone="primary" size="sm">Новое</Badge>
```

`tone="accent"` (turquoise) for AI/highlight callouts, `tone="primary"` (blue) for brand emphasis, `tone="neutral"` for quiet tags. Pairs well pinned to the top-right corner of `PricingCard`.
